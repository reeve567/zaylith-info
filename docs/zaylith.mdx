# Zaylith

Zaylith is a female barbarian adventurer with an Irish accent.
She is a tall, muscular tiefling with white hair.

She has a pet, Kavik, a griffin.
She bought land and a shed for him to live in.

## History

Zaylith was a former soldier in war, but she volunteer instead of being drafted.
She thought she could make a change in the world, and it's not known whether she was successful.

Her first mission was as a spy, to save a chancellor.
